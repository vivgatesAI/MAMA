What is Graph RAG?

1

Smart Chunking

Documents split into 1000-token chunks with 200-token overlap. Breaks at sentence/paragraph boundaries to preserve context.

2

Entity Extraction

Automatically identifies drugs, genes, proteins, diseases, biomarkers, and clinical trials using pattern matching.

3

Knowledge Graph

Chunks sharing entities are linked. Enables multi-hop traversal to find related information across documents.

4

Hybrid Retrieval

Combines vector similarity (semantic) with graph traversal (structural) for superior context retrieval.
