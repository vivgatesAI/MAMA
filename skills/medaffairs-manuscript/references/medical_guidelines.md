# Medical Writing Guidelines for MAMA

## Key Standards
- **GPP3** (Good Publication Practice): Transparency, accountability, ethical practices
- **ICMJE** Recommendations: Authorship criteria, conflicts of interest disclosure
- **CONSORT** for clinical trials
- **PRISMA** for systematic reviews
- Avoid promotional language, overstatement of efficacy/safety, unsubstantiated claims
